About Us

WHO WE ARE

Tonia's Food Hub got it's name from my favorite sister(Anthonia) who is a foodie. We offer Nigerian dishes, drinks, salads and assorted meats in a sleek and upscale environment. We are here to support your healthy lifestyle through the use of fresh, organic and raw foods.

WHAT WE ARE ABOUT

At Tonia's Food_Hub we are all about making our customers happy, as we have the belief that everyone deserves access to real Nigerian delicacies. We provide a variety of dishes that best serves our customers and make them happy. We create Nigerian food that we are proud to serve and deliver it fast, with a smile.
